    Getting to Our Wedding

    Come away with us to Paris, where romance happens.

    Eurostar

    If you are coming from London, we strongly recommend travelling by Eurostar. Book in advance for cheaper tickets. Unlimited luggage space. Three hours from Kings Cross St. Pancras International Train Station to Gare du Nord. Multiple trains daily. Book your seats here. 

    ​

    CDG Airport

    If you are coming from Hong Kong or anywhere else abroad, your flight will be likely to arrive in the CDG Airport. Journey from CDG to the Paris city centre takes about 40 minutes via the express train or by car. Find out more here. 

    By car

    If you are a driving enthusiast like Derek, driving from central London to the city centre of Paris will only take you 5 hours door-to-door. This includes waiting, queuing, journey time in the Eurotunnel, etc. Just remember to drive on the right side when you're in Paris and left when in London! Information on Eurotunnel here. 

    ​

    Getting around town
    Most major sights will be close to a Metro station - spot the logo "M" that resembles McDonald's. We recommend taking Uber, especially if you are travelling in a group of two or more! Just download the app and link your credit card to it. Or you can even pay in cash! (Waiting for marketing fee from Uber...)
